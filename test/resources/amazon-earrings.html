Ornami

The Ornami jewellery collection is inspired by the international language of Esperanto.

Ornami is the romantic Latin Esperanto word for adorn and jewellery is worn to enhance style.

Adorn someone special with an Ornami jewellery piece. Design inspiration and influence is taken from around the world, creating a variety of styles. The collection offers choices for all occasions and tastes.

A collection of jewellery accessories, crafted in sterling silver and alternative metals, many set with precious and semi precious gem stones.

All items presented in a gift box and with a branded jewellery tag.
